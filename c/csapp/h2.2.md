# homework 2.2 power index

## question
fill 2 power index

n  |2^n(dec)|2^n(hex)
---|--------|--------
9  |512     |0x200
19 |?       |?
?  |16384   |?
?  |?       |0x10000
17 |?       |?
?  |32      |?
?  |?       |0x80

## answer

n  |2^n(dec)|2^n(hex)
---|--------|--------
9  |512     |0x200
19 |524288  |0x80000
14 |16384   |0x4000
16 |65536   |0x10000
17 |131072  |0x20000
5  |32      |0x20
7  |128     |0x80

## hint
every 2^4 = 0x10

and ^0=0x1, ^1=0x2, ^2=0x4, ^3=0x8

so n = i + 4j, i is first number, j is numbers of 0.
