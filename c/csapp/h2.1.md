# homework 2.1 hex & bin transition

## question
1. 0x39A7F8                    to bin
2. 1100 1001 0111 1011         to hex
3. 0xD5E4C                     to bin
4. 10 0110 1110 0111 1011 0101 to hex

## awnser
1. 0011 1001 1010 0111 1111 0100
2. 0xC97B
3. 1101 0101 1110 0100 1100
4. 0x3AE7B5

## hint
change form

bin |dec|hex|bin |dec|hex
----|---|---|----|---|---
0000|0  |0  |1000|8  |8
0001|1  |1  |1001|9  |9
0010|2  |2  |1010|10 |A
0011|3  |3  |1011|11 |B
0100|4  |4  |1100|12 |C
0101|5  |5  |1101|13 |D
0110|6  |6  |1110|14 |E
0111|7  |7  |1111|15 |F

remember 7 to 0111, A to 1010, C to 1100
